Purulia

The Oldest District of Bengal

It is on the west part of the West Bengal. The landscape is rocky and undulating. Here you find hills, forests as well as it's archeological excavations and the relics of ancient buildings and temples. Tribal ethos enriches the mystic charms and natural beauty of this land of red soil and red blooms of Palash. What sets apart the Purulia style from others is the use of Chhau masks. One of the best places to watch the making of masks is Charida village at the base of Ajodhya hill.
