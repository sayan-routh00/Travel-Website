Mousuni Island

In The Lap of Nature

It is a offbeat virgin sea beach near kolkata. It's beauty lies in the confluence of the sea with the river. Mousuni Island is a nature enthusiast and photographer's delight. The panoramic sea view of Baliara beach, the riverine landscape of Patibunia, the variety of birds in the Kakramarir chor, the simple rural life, the mesmerising sunset - all this and more make up the Mousuni Island picture album. You can enjoy good time with friends and family far from city busy life. You can boat ride, night spend in tent, enjoy bonfire and have barbeque on demand.
