Murshidabad

The Unknown Wealth Capital of Bengal

It named after Murshid Kuli Khan, the Diwan of Bengal. It was the capital of Bengal since 1704 and it continued to be the capital under British Raj untill 1790. Murshidabad is famous for brass and bell metal ware, for traditional bengali muslim and silk weaves, brocades and Jamdanis. There are plenty of shops offering mouthwatering bengali sweets to take home for family and friends.
